About Me

Get to know who I am!

I'm a high school senior at Goochland High School with a passion for software development, robotics, and problem-solving. My journey in technology started when I joined my high school's FIRST Robotics team as an 8th grader during the 2020-2021 school year.


As the Programming Lead for our robotics team, I've developed expertise in Java and Kotlin, created vision systems using odometry and fiducials, and integrated mechanical systems with software. My work with the team earned me the FIRST Dean's List Semi-Finalist Award, recognizing my technical contributions and leadership.


Beyond robotics, I've expanded my skills in various programming languages and technologies, including Python, Flutter/Dart, C++, and web development. I enjoy working on diverse projects, from mobile apps to 3D printing solutions for my community.


I'm pursuing an AAS in Social Sciences at Reynolds Community College while completing my high school education. With a GPA of 4.07 and a growing portfolio of technical projects, I'm excited about the future possibilities in software development and engineering.
